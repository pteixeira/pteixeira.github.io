My name is Pedro Adriano Pessoa Teixeira and I am 28 years old.
I was born and raised in Porto, Portugal, where I got my M. Sc. in Informatics and Computing Engineering at the Faculty of Engineering of University of Porto (FEUP).
In this course I gained a great interest in Web and mobile development, mostly through the various projects I was required to do.
I spent 13 years learning English in The British Council, where I got the CPE (Certificate of Proficiency in English) and received a "B" grade, amongst other certificates.
I have also spent a little bit over a year learning Japanese and received the level 10 Japan Kanji Aptitude Test certificate.
